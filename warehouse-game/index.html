Reading an address. ROW 1–10 are the shelf units, numbered from receiving. Each unit has BAY 1 on its front face (toward receiving) and BAY 2 on its back face (toward the north walkway). SUBSECTIONS 1–8 run west → east and are stenciled on the floor. Inspecting a face shows SHELF LEVELS 1 (bottom) – 3 (top), each holding BOXES 1–7.

The sign at the top of the screen always shows the zone, row, bay and subsection you are standing at.

No timer, no fail state, nothing chasing you. Sound is off by default; a reduced-motion mode is in Settings. Reset (top right) returns you to receiving and empties the inventory.
