# Challenge 1 — Photo Day (lvl 2)

## Intercept report

Our listening post pulled a private email off the wire last night: the
self-styled **Secretary of Watermelon** writing to his contact, **Mr. Tema**.
The Secretary has been bragging that a "nerd from the intelligence agency"
taught him how to move secrets in plain sight, and this message — subject line
*"Photo Message!"* — is the first traffic we've captured since.

On the surface it's nothing: small talk, questionable medical decisions, and a
photograph of a honey badger. Command doesn't buy it. Nobody attaches a badger
photo for no reason.

## Your tasking

You have been handed the intercepted message exactly as captured:

- `email.eml`

Analyze the intercept. Everything the Secretary sent is in that one file —
read what he wrote, and take a very close look at what he attached. If he is
hiding a message, recover it.

## Deliverable

The hidden message is a flag in the format `Flag{...}`. Submit the exact
string, including capitalization and punctuation.

*Good hunting. And remember: the Secretary is not nearly as clever as he
thinks he is.*
